Privacy & Policies

Important legal and privacy information for Kaizen Automation Hub customers.

Privacy Policy

We collect and process user data for the purpose of providing services such as voice calls, messaging, and IoT telemetry. Personal data is stored securely with role-based access and encryption at rest and in transit. Data retention periods are configurable per contract and we support data deletion requests under applicable law.

  • Data minimization and purpose limitation
  • Encryption in transit & at rest
  • Access controls & auditing
  • Data subject rights and deletion

Terms of Use

Use of Kaizen services requires a signed agreement. Customers must ensure they have lawful rights to contact recipients and must comply with relevant telecom regulations. Kaizen reserves the right to suspend services for abuse or illegal use.

  • Service descriptions and SLAs are provided in the contract
  • Customer is responsible for consent where required
  • Payment terms, cancellations, and termination policies apply

Refund Policy

Monthly subscriptions may be canceled anytime; refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. For enterprise engagements, refund and dispute terms are defined in the commercial agreement.

Data Protection

We follow industry-standard best practices for data protection. Customers can request data export in machine-readable formats. If a data breach occurs, affected customers will be notified according to applicable law and contractual obligations.

AI Usage Policy

Kaizen provides tools to build AI-driven communications. We require customers not to use services for activities that are illegal, discriminatory, or intended to deceive. Automated political persuasion and manipulative deepfakes are not permitted without explicit agreement and disclosures.

  • No illegal or harmful content
  • Transparency for synthetic voices when required by law
  • Respect for opt-outs and do-not-call lists
